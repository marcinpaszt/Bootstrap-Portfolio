About me

My name is Marcin. I am 30 years old and I was born in Poland, in 1987, during the late years of communism. In the early 90s, I remember being very minimalistic and being taught by my parents to appreciate everything I had.

I moved to America in August of 2001, a month before the 9/11 attacks on World Trade Center. Originally, I was only going to remain here for 3 years but I stayed an extra 12 years due to the fact that an American college degree looks great everywhere else in the world.

I love music, especially heavy metal. My favoroite band is Rammstein, from Germany. I enjoy going to concerts and attempt to buy some merchandise from every concert I go to.

I also like video games and my favorite is The Witcher 3, not just because it was made by a Polish studio.

I like good beer and pizza.
